To enjoy the best experience with Vortex make sure your Internet connection will be able to support cloud gaming. We recommend that you have at least 10Mbps connection. 5Ghz Wi-Fi and 4G/LTE is highly recommended for stable wireless connection.

To experience the highest possible gaming quality make sure your bandwidth is not overloaded by other applications. For better performance close additional tabs in your browser and all services that may use Internet connection (e.g. Facebook, YouToube, etc.)

Not sure if your connection will support GameOver? Just use our connection test and be sure!

NOTE: This test will help you check if your connection is good enough to play via GameOver. To get the most accurate results please perform it again on the device you plan using GameOver on. The results may vary between devices.
